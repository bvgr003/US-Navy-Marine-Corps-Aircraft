AH-1Z Viper

The AH-1Z Viper is an advanced twin-engine attack helicopter developed for the United States Marine Corps. It features a four-blade, bearingless, composite main rotor system, upgraded avionics and sensors, and a powerful new engine. The Viper is equipped with state-of-the-art weapons systems, including AGM-114 Hellfire missiles, Hydra 70 rocket pods, and a 20mm cannon. Its advanced targeting systems and digital cockpit provide enhanced situational awareness and mission effectiveness. The AH-1Z is designed for close air support, armed reconnaissance, and anti-armor warfare, making it a versatile and lethal asset on the modern battlefield.
