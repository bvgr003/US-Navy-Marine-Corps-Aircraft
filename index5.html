SH-60 Seahawk

The SH-60 Seahawk is a versatile maritime helicopter used primarily by the United States Navy for anti-submarine warfare (ASW) and search and rescue (SAR) missions. It is a twin-engine helicopter with a four-bladed main rotor and a tail rotor, providing excellent maneuverability and stability during operations over water. The Seahawk is equipped with advanced sensors and systems, including radar, sonar, and electronic warfare suites, allowing it to detect and track submarines and other surface targets. Additionally, it can be configured for troop transport, medical evacuation, and utility missions, making it a valuable asset for naval operations worldwide.
