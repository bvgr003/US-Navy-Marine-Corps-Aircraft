UH-1Y Venom

The UH-1Y Venom is a versatile utility helicopter used by the United States Marine Corps. It is a modernized version of the iconic UH-1 Huey, featuring a four-blade, composite rotor system, upgraded avionics, and a more powerful engine. The Venom is used for a variety of missions, including troop transport, medical evacuation, and close air support. It can carry up to 13 fully-equipped troops or six stretchers, making it a valuable asset in combat and humanitarian operations. The UH-1Y is equipped with advanced weapons systems, including door-mounted machine guns and rocket pods, enhancing its firepower and battlefield effectiveness. Its ability to operate in diverse environments and provide reliable support to ground forces makes it a critical component of the Marine Corps' aviation fleet.
